Development Bot: This bot would prioritize moving its pieces from their starting
positions, focusing on developing minor pieces (knights and bishops) early in
the game. Space Bot: This bot would focus on controlling as much of the board as
possible, advancing its pawns to gain space and restrict the opponent's pieces.
Tempo Bot: A bot that tries to gain a "tempo" by making moves that force the
opponent to react, thereby controlling the flow of the game. Pawn Structure Bot:
This bot's logic would revolve around creating a strong pawn structure, avoiding
doubled pawns, and creating pawn chains. Blockade Bot: This bot would focus on
blockading the opponent's pawns and pieces, restricting their mobility.
